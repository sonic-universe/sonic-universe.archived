Welcome To Our Server!

This is the Sonic Universe RP Server. If you have cool Sonic OCs or want to be the existing characters, this server is the one for you.
 
OC Registration and Balancing: Our OC registration system insists of a reference sheet that you must copy and paste. Just fill out the information and submit it. We will have someone check it as soon as possible. If your OC has powers and/or skills that are too OP, we can chat and help you fix it. We always make sure to keep things fair for everyone.
 
Character Requests: We offer all users a chance to roleplay as their favorite Sonic characters from the games or the Archie Comics. Just pick a character and make a request. If it is available, we will have you do an audition to make sure you can fit the part. If you do good, you get the part. If not, feel free to pick another and try again.
 
Outside of RP: We offer many forms of entertainment outside of the RP including channels for general chat, art, videos, memes, blogspam, and music players. There will always be something to do.
 
Suggestions and Concerns: We always take advice from members to make the server better. If you have any ideas or concerns, feel free to let us know.
 
The server server always works hard to to help the server improve, and opinions from it's members always helps. So feel free to check it out. We'll see you there.

The (Online) Server Staff

The Official Server Rules

  • Be Respectful. Bullying, Pressuring, and Any Form of Disrespect to Staff or other Members will get you kicked.
  • Keep Drama out of the Server. If you need to vent, use the proper channel. Contact a Staff Member if you need to talk about something personal.
  • Do not Share/Link to any Malicious Links or Content. This includes Viruses, IP or Cookie Grabbers, Malware, Harmware, etc. Doing so will result in a Permanent Ban, Immediately.
  • Refrain from Spamming. Don't repeat your Messages, Share Chain Letters/Messages, or anything annoying of the sort.
  • Abusing Pings or Members will result in a Ban. This includes using @everyone or Pinging Staff without good reason, or Harassing another Member.
  • Post Content in the Correct Channels. Helps keep everything organized and tidy.
  • Do not post your Sensitive Personal Information. This includes Passwords, Pins, SSN, Etc. Report anyone who asks for Personal Info. This rule is for your safety, No one needs to know those things but you.
  • Staff's Words are Law. Abide by the Rules and Listen when they Mention You.
  • No NSFW or +18 content. Doing so will get you Kicked and your Post Deleted. We do allow love and ships between characters, but you must keep it reasonable.
  • Be respectful to the staff on the server. The Head Admins, Secondary Admins, and Moderators work really hard to keep things fair, balanced, and fun. We will not tolerate harassment, aggressiveness, or hostility towards the staff. These offenses will result in a warning, then a ban if necessary.
  • No secretive harassment or flaming staff in PMs. If you try to flame or harass any of the staff on the server, you will get an immediate ban. If you must speak your mind to a staff member privately, try to do it without malicious force or with the or make it seem like they don't care about the server or it's members.
